Welcome to SuttaCentral Voice

SC-Voice is a website for the study of Early Buddhist Texts (EBT) from SuttaCentral for the visually disabled and the sighted. It allows for reading and listening to the texts. It hosts root texts in Pali and translations into many modern languages.

Find instructions on how to search the Wiki section here.
